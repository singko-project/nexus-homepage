WELCOME TO NEXUS

Nexus is your members-only platform designed to connect buyers, sellers, affiliate marketers, and content creators in one community-driven space. As a member, you gain access to tools, opportunities, and exclusive benefits that help you grow, share, and engage — plus enjoy our free promotional raffle and referral program as a bonus.

MEMBERSHIP

1. Membership Includes
As a member of Nexus, you gain access to a vibrant all-in-one community where you can:
• Buy, sell, or promote products and services.
• Share your affiliate links.
• Showcase your channel as a content creator.
• Connect with members to learn, collaborate, and grow your network.
• Support others by purchasing their products, services, or following their social media channels.
• Participate in our free promotional raffle.
• Join the referral program to earn rewards.
• Track your account, total referral bonuses earned, bonus raffle entries, and view draw results.

2. Membership Validity
• Membership is valid for one (1) month starting from the completion of the raffle draw.
•There is no renewal system because each batch starts fresh. When the next batch opens, everything resets — the community, referral codes, raffle entries, and access to all tools.
•If you wish to join the next cycle, simply wait for the announcement or invitation for the upcoming batch.

FREE RAFFLE

• The Free Raffle is a bonus benefit for all Nexus members — designed to reward your participation and engagement in the community.
• As a member, you automatically receive a unique initial raffle number. To make the experience more exciting and give members a higher chance of winning, you can boost your chances without any additional cost by promoting the Nexus platform to others.
• Each new member you bring in grants 1 additional bonus raffle entry. The more you share, the better your chances of winning — ensuring everyone has a fair and rewarding opportunity.

Fair and Transparent Draw
• The draw begins once the Promo End Timer reaches 100%.
• Raffle winners are selected randomly using our advanced Raffle Number Generator. The process is transparent, unbiased, and designed to make every draw exciting.
• To keep yourself updated on whether the draw has started, please visit the Promo End Timer. You can also view your unique initial raffle number, bonus raffle entries, and referral code, and check the Raffle Winners inside the Account Lookup page.

REFERRAL PROGRAM

The Referral Program is designed to reward members for helping grow the Nexus community. By inviting others to join, you not only contribute to a thriving, engaging platform but also enjoy extra benefits for yourself.

Additional Bonus Raffle Entries
For every successful referral you bring into Nexus, you receive 1 extra raffle entry for the Free Raffle.
• These entries are added automatically to your account once the referral joins and is verified.
• There is no limit to how many bonus entries you can earn — the more people you refer, the higher your chances of winning in the Free Raffle.
• Bonus entries stack on top of your initial raffle entry, giving you an advantage without any additional cost.
• This system ensures that all members have a fair and transparent opportunity to win, while also encouraging growth and engagement within the community.

Referral Bonus
As part of the Referral Program, each successful member you bring into Nexus earns you a ₱100 referral bonus
. • The Referral bonus is automatically processed once the system detects your referral and will be sent directly to your e-wallet.
• There is no limit to how many referral bonuses you can earn — the more members you invite, the more rewards you accumulate.
• Referral bonuses are separate from bonus raffle entries, giving you both monetary rewards and higher chances of winning in the Free Raffle.
